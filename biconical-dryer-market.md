<p><p>The biconical dryer market consists of two main types: atmospheric dryers and vacuum dryers. Atmospheric dryers operate at ambient pressure, allowing for efficient drying of heat-sensitive materials by utilizing hot air. In contrast, vacuum dryers reduce the pressure within the drying chamber, facilitating lower-temperature drying processes that minimize product degradation and enhance drying efficiency. Both types cater to various industries, including pharmaceuticals and food processing, where precise control over drying conditions is crucial for product quality.</p></p>
