<p><p>Articulated Dump Trucks (ADTs) are specialized off-road vehicles designed to transport heavy loads over rough and uneven terrains, making them ideal for agriculture and forestry applications. These trucks feature a pivot joint between the cab and the dump bed, providing enhanced maneuverability and stability on challenging surfaces. In agriculture, ADTs are used for tasks such as transporting crops, fertilizers, and equipment, while in forestry, they assist in moving logs, debris, and other materials.</p><p>The Articulated Dump Trucks for Agriculture & Forestry Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
