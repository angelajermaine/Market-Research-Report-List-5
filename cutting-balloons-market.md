<p><strong>The Cutting Balloons Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Compliant Balloons</li><li>Non-Compliant Balloons</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting balloons market is categorized into two main types: semi-compliant balloons and non-compliant balloons. Semi-compliant balloons are designed to expand under pressure while maintaining a controlled diameter, allowing for effective cutting during vascular procedures without significantly damaging surrounding tissues. Non-compliant balloons, on the other hand, maintain a fixed diameter and do not expand significantly, providing precise cutting capabilities. Both types cater to different medical needs, ensuring optimal performance in various interventional procedures.</p></p>
