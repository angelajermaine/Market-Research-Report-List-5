<p><p>Ultrasonic distance measuring sensors are categorized based on their measurement configurations: cylindrical and rectangular. Cylindrical sensors are typically used in applications requiring a compact, uniform shape, making them ideal for tight spaces or rounded surfaces. In contrast, rectangular sensors offer a larger surface area, accommodating broader measurement ranges and providing greater stability. Both types leverage ultrasonic waves to accurately gauge distances, enabling their use in automation, robotics, and industrial applications where precise spatial awareness is crucial.</p></p>
