<p><strong>The Real-time Mocap System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical System</li><li>Non-optical System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The real-time motion capture (mocap) system market is divided into two main types: optical and non-optical systems. Optical systems use cameras and markers to capture motion by tracking the reflected light from the markers placed on the subject. In contrast, non-optical systems utilize technologies like inertial sensors or magnetic fields to capture motion without the need for visual markers. Each type offers unique advantages, such as precision in optical systems and portability in non-optical systems, catering to various application needs.</p></p>
