<p><p>MOOCs, or Massive Open Online Courses, are categorized into two main market types: xMOOCs and cMOOCs. xMOOCs primarily focus on traditional instructional models, featuring structured content, assessments, and a predefined schedule, often delivered by universities. In contrast, cMOOCs emphasize a learner-centered approach, fostering collaboration, interaction, and knowledge creation among participants. This type encourages open dialogue and community-driven learning, relying on social media and online forums. Together, they represent diverse approaches to online education, catering to various learning preferences.</p></p>
