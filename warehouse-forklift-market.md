Electric forklifts are powered by batteries, offering quieter operation and zero emissions, making them ideal for indoor use. In contrast, internal combustion forklifts rely on fuel sources like gasoline or diesel, providing higher lifting capacities and greater outdoor versatility. Both types serve distinct operational needs, with electric models favored for sustainability and indoor applications, while internal combustion forklifts excel in rugged, outdoor environments.</p></p>
