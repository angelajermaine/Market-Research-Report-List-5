<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spandex Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solution Dry Spinning</li><li>Solution Wet Spinning</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spandex fiber market comprises various production methods, including solution dry spinning, solution wet spinning, and others. </p><p>Solution dry spinning involves dissolving the spandex polymer in a solvent and then extruding it through spinnerets into heated air, allowing the solvent to evaporate and form fibers. </p><p>Solution wet spinning, on the other hand, dissolves the polymer in a solvent and extrudes it into a coagulating bath, which solidifies the fibers. </p><p>Other methods may include variations or hybrid techniques that amalgamate these processes for specialized applications.</p></p>
