<p><p>Molten salt solar energy thermal storage is a technology that captures thermal energy from the sun, using molten salt as a heat transfer and storage medium. This energy can be stored and utilized when sunlight is unavailable. Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) encompasses various systems, such as Parabolic Trough Systems, which use curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver; Power Tower Systems, with a central tower and multiple mirrors; and Dish/Engine Systems, which convert heat directly to electricity via a Stirling engine. Each system offers unique advantages in efficiency and scalability.</p></p>
