<p><strong>The Wide Format Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LargeFlatbed Scanner</li><li>Feed-through scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wide format scanner market can be categorized into two main types: large flatbed scanners and feed-through scanners. Large flatbed scanners feature a flat surface where documents and images are placed for scanning, providing high-quality results and accommodating various media sizes. On the other hand, feed-through scanners, also known as roll scanners, utilize a feed mechanism to continuously scan large formats like plans and maps, allowing for faster throughput and efficiency, particularly in high-volume scanning environments.</p></p>
