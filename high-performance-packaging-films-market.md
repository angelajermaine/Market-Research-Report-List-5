<p><p>High-performance packaging films encompass various types, each serving specific applications. CPE (chlorinated polyethylene) films are known for their flexibility and resistance to chemicals. ONY (Oriented Nylon) films offer excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases, making them ideal for food packaging. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) films are lightweight, providing clarity and strength. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) films are versatile with good heat sealability. AL (Aluminum) films deliver superior protection against light and oxygen. Other materials include specialized films for diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
