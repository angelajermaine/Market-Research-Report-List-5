<p><p>The Audio System on Chip (SoC) market comprises two main types: analog and digital. Analog audio SoCs primarily handle sound processing through continuous signals, ensuring high fidelity in audio reproduction, typically used in traditional audio applications. On the other hand, digital audio SoCs convert sound waves into digital signals for processing, enabling advanced features like noise reduction, wireless communication, and integration with smart devices. Both types cater to various applications, ranging from consumer electronics to professional audio equipment.</p></p>
