<p><p>The GaN UV sensor market is segmented into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors detect long wavelengths (320-400 nm) and are commonly used in applications like skincare and phototherapy. UVB sensors, operating in the 280-320 nm range, are crucial for monitoring sun exposure and assessing risks of skin damage. UVC sensors target shorter wavelengths (100-280 nm) and are primarily utilized in disinfection and sterilization processes. Each type serves distinct applications, driving demand across various industries.</p></p>
