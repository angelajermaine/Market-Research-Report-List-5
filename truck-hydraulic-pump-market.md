<p><p>The truck hydraulic pump market primarily features two main types: hydraulic gear pumps and hydraulic dump pumps. Hydraulic gear pumps are designed to provide steady flow and pressure by using gears to move hydraulic fluid, making them ideal for various truck applications. In contrast, hydraulic dump pumps are specifically engineered for operating dump truck systems, allowing for efficient lifting and lowering of cargo beds. Both types play critical roles in enhancing the functionality and efficiency of hydraulic systems in trucks.</p></p>
