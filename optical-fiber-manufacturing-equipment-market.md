<p><p>The optical fiber manufacturing equipment market is segmented into several key systems. The core deposition system focuses on creating the fiber's central core, while the clad deposition system applies cladding to enhance light transmission. Core and clad sintering systems are responsible for solidifying these layers, ensuring structural integrity. The preform soaking system eliminates any impurities in the preform, enhancing fiber quality. Lastly, the rod draw system enables the transformation of preforms into thin optical fibers, integral for telecommunications and data transmission industries.</p></p>
