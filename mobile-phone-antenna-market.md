<p><p>The mobile phone antenna market includes various types, each serving specific needs. Stamping antennas are manufactured using metal sheet techniques, offering durability and compactness. FPC (Flexible Printed Circuit) antennas are lightweight and easily integrated into devices, enhancing design flexibility. LDS (Laser Direct Structuring) antennas employ advanced laser technology for precise 3D structures, ideal for complex designs. LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) antennas provide excellent dielectric properties and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-frequency applications in modern smartphones.</p></p>
