<p><p>The ultra-thin fiber cement board market is categorized into three density types: high, medium, and low. High-density boards offer superior durability and strength, making them suitable for high-impact applications and areas requiring moisture resistance. Medium-density boards strike a balance between strength and weight, often used for general construction purposes. Low-density boards are lightweight, making them ideal for applications needing ease of handling and installation, though they may offer less durability than their higher-density counterparts. Each type addresses specific needs in construction and design.</p></p>
