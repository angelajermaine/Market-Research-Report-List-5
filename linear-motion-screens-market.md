<p><p>Linear motion screens are essential in various industries for efficient material separation and grading. The market is typically divided into two types: single deck and double deck screens. Single deck screens are designed for simpler applications, processing one layer of material, making them ideal for quick sorting tasks. In contrast, double deck screens offer enhanced efficiency by processing two layers simultaneously, allowing for finer separation and increased throughput. Both types cater to diverse needs in mining, construction, and recycling sectors.</p></p>
