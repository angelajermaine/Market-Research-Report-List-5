<p><strong>The Synthetic Polymer Hemodialysis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic polymer hemodialysis membrane market encompasses two primary types: low flux membranes and high flux membranes. </p><p>Low flux membranes are designed for patients with stable creatinine levels, allowing smaller molecules to pass while effectively removing waste. In contrast, high flux membranes are suitable for patients requiring extensive clearance, facilitating the removal of larger molecules and solutes, thus enhancing overall dialysis efficiency. The choice between these membranes largely depends on the patient's clinical needs and the desired dialysis treatment outcomes.</p></p>
