<p><p>The varifocal eyeglass lenses market consists of two primary types: freely-varifocal lenses and multifocal lenses. Freely-varifocal lenses offer a continuous gradient of vision correction, allowing wearers to see clearly at various distances without distinct zones, making them ideal for dynamic vision needs. Multifocal lenses, on the other hand, have defined segments for near, intermediate, and far vision, providing clear focus for each distance but with noticeable transitions. Both types cater to different vision correction requirements and lifestyle preferences.</p></p>
