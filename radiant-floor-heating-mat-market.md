<p><p>Radiant floor heating mats are electrical heating systems that are installed under floors to provide consistent and efficient warmth throughout a space. These systems utilize electric heating cables or mats that produce radiant heat, warming the floor surface and, in turn, the surrounding air. This method of heating offers advantages such as energy efficiency, improved comfort, and the elimination of cold spots, making it popular in residential and commercial settings.</p><p>The Radiant Floor Heating Mat Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period. The market is also experiencing a surge in renovation activities and new construction projects that emphasize comfort and energy efficiency, further fueling growth.
