<p><p>The oilfield chemicals and surfactants market comprises various types based on their charge characteristics. Anionic surfactants are negatively charged and enhance oil recovery by reducing surface tension. Non-ionic surfactants lack charge and provide stability in diverse conditions. Cationic surfactants are positively charged and are effective in controlling clay swelling and stabilization. Amphoteric surfactants can act as either anionic or cationic depending on the environment. Other chemicals include specialty products tailored for specific applications in oilfield operations.</p></p>
