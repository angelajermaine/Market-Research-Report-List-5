<p><strong>The Magnesium Oxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM)</li><li>Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM)</li><li>Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM)</li><li>Synthetic Magnesium Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium oxide market is categorized into various types based on production methods and characteristics. Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM) is produced at high temperatures, offering high reactivity and is used in refractory applications. Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM) is created by melting magnesium oxide and is known for its high purity and strength in metallurgical processes. Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM) is derived from magnesium hydroxide and is utilized in various chemicals. Synthetic Magnesium Oxide is produced artificially, providing specific properties for specialized applications.</p></p>
