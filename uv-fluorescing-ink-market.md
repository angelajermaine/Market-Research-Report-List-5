<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV fluorescing ink market encompasses two primary types: solvent-based and water-based inks. Solvent-based inks use organic solvents as a carrier, providing strong adhesion and vibrant colors, making them suitable for various applications like packaging and industrial printing. Water-based inks, on the other hand, utilize water as a solvent, offering a more environmentally friendly option with low VOC emissions. They are ideal for flexible substrates and are often preferred for eco-conscious printing solutions, ensuring both performance and sustainability.</p></p>
