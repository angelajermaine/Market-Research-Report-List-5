<p><p>The disposable surgical stapler market is divided into two main types: linear disposable surgical staplers and circular disposable surgical staplers. Linear staplers are designed for straight incisions, typically used in procedures like gastrointestinal surgeries, where they create a line of staples to close tissues. Circular staplers, on the other hand, are utilized for circular anastomosis procedures, enabling surgeons to connect tubular structures like the intestine. Both types offer precision and efficiency, enhancing surgical outcomes while minimizing the risk of infection.</p></p>
