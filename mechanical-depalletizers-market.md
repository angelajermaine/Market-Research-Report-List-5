<p><ul><li>Conventional Depalletizers</li><li>Robotic Depalletizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical depalletizers market is divided into two main types: conventional depalletizers and robotic depalletizers. Conventional depalletizers utilize mechanical systems, such as pushers and grippers, to remove layers of products from pallets efficiently, making them suitable for high-speed operations. In contrast, robotic depalletizers leverage robotic arms equipped with specialized end-effectors, offering flexibility to handle various product shapes and sizes. This technology provides enhanced precision and adaptability, often reducing labor costs and increasing productivity in modern manufacturing environments.</p></p>
