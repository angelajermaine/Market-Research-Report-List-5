<p><p>The Unmanned Marine Vehicles market comprises two primary types: Remotely Operated Surface Vehicles (ROSVs) and Autonomous Surface Vehicles (ASVs). ROSVs are controlled from a distance, allowing operators to execute specific tasks in real-time, making them ideal for inspection, surveillance, and research missions. In contrast, ASVs operate independently using onboard sensors and software, enabling them to navigate and perform tasks without direct human control, which is advantageous for long-duration missions like environmental monitoring and data collection.</p></p>
