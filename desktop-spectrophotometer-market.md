<p><p>The Desktop Spectrophotometer market comprises various types, each serving distinct analytical purposes. Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers analyze elemental compositions by measuring light absorbance, ideal for metals in samples. Fluorescence Spectrophotometers quantify compounds based on their fluorescence properties. UV-visible Spectrophotometers measure absorbance in the ultraviolet-visible spectrum, useful in various chemical analyses. Infrared Spectrophotometers analyze molecular vibrations, aiding in identifying functional groups in compounds. Visible Spectrophotometers focus on the visible spectrum, commonly used in colorimetric analysis. Each type caters to specific research and industrial needs.</p></p>
