<p><p>Low Dielectric Glass Fibre Market includes two main types: D-Glass Fiber and NE-Glass Fiber. </p><p>D-Glass Fiber, also known as alkali-lime glass, is a common type of glass fiber with high dielectric strength and low dielectric constant. It is widely used in applications where electrical insulation is required, such as in the telecommunications and electronics industries. </p><p>NE-Glass Fiber, also known as non-alkali glass, is another type of glass fiber that offers similar electrical properties to D-Glass Fiber but with improved resistance to high temperatures and chemicals.</p></p>
