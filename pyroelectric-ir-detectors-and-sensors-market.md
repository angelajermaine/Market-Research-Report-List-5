<p><p>Pyroelectric infrared (IR) detectors and sensors are classified into several types based on their configuration and functionality. Single-element detectors consist of a single pyroelectric crystal and are ideal for applications requiring simple temperature variations detection. Dual-element detectors feature two crystals, enhancing sensitivity and enabling differential temperature measurement, often used in security systems. Other types may include linear arrays or imaging systems that offer more complex detection capabilities, catering to advanced applications in industries like automotive and consumer electronics.</p></p>
