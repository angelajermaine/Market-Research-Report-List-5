<p><p>The Lightning Current Shunt market encompasses various types tailored for specific frequency applications. High Frequency Current Shunts are designed for rapid transient current measurement, ideal for applications involving fast pulsed signals. IF Current Shunts cater to intermediate frequency ranges, typically used in communication systems. Low Frequency Current Shunts are optimized for monitoring lower frequency currents, suitable for power systems and grounding applications. Each type serves distinct needs, ensuring accurate and reliable lightning current measurements across different frequency domains.</p></p>
