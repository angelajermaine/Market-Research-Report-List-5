<p><p>Compression stockings, also known as elastic stockings, are designed to improve circulation and reduce swelling in the legs. The market includes various types, notably gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks provide graduated compression, being tighter at the ankle and gradually loosening towards the knee, promoting blood flow. Anti-embolism socks, on the other hand, are specifically designed to prevent deep vein thrombosis in immobile patients, offering consistent support throughout the leg. Both types serve crucial roles in venous health and patient care.</p></p>
