Biopsies entail extracting tissue samples for histological examination, allowing for direct detection of fungi within affected tissues. Medical imaging utilizes techniques like CT or MRI scans to visualize internal structures and assess infection spread. Together, these methods enhance the accuracy and speed of diagnosing fungal infections, crucial for effective treatment.</p></p>
