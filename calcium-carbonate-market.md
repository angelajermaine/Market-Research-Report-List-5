<p><ul><li>Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC)</li><li>Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Calcium Carbonate market primarily consists of two types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is produced by mechanically crushing limestone and is commonly used in construction, paper, and plastic industries due to its cost-effectiveness and availability. In contrast, PCC is synthesized chemically, offering a purer form with controlled particle size, making it ideal for specialized applications like pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics. Both types serve distinct industrial needs and contribute significantly to various sectors.</p></p>
