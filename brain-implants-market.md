<p><p>The brain implants market consists of various types, including Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS), Spinal Cord Stimulation (SCS), and Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS). DBS involves implanting electrodes in specific brain areas to treat conditions like Parkinson's disease. SCS uses implanted devices to deliver electrical impulses to the spinal cord, alleviating chronic pain. VNS involves stimulating the vagus nerve to manage epilepsy and depression. Each type offers targeted therapeutic benefits and is evolving with advancements in technology and neuroengineering.</p></p>
