<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Extensometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-Type Extensometers</li><li>Non Contact-Type Extensometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optic Extensometers market features two primary types: contact-type and non-contact-type extensometers. Contact-type extensometers involve physical contact with the measuring surface, using fiber optics to assess deformation and displacement accurately. In contrast, non-contact-type extensometers utilize laser or optical techniques to measure changes without direct contact, allowing for greater flexibility and reduced potential for damage. Both types are essential in monitoring structural health in various industries, including civil engineering, geotechnical applications, and aerospace.</p></p>
