<p><p>Float switch sensors are devices used to detect the level of liquids in tanks or other vessels by utilizing a float mechanism. Special type float switches include customized designs for specific applications, offering unique features like resistance to harsh environments or multiple switch points, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
