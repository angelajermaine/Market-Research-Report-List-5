<p><p>Polymer-Dispersed Liquid Crystals (PDLCs) are a unique blend of liquid crystals and polymers that exhibit variable optical properties. The market includes Scattering PDLCs, which scatter light when an electric field is absent, transforming from opaque to transparent when activated. Nano-PDLCs utilize nanoparticles for enhanced performance and improved switching speeds. Polymer Network Liquid Crystals combine liquid crystal droplets within a polymer matrix, providing excellent mechanical stability and tunable optical characteristics. Together, these innovations drive advancements in display technologies and smart windows.</p></p>
