<p><p>Serial EEPROM (Electrically Erasable Programmable Read-Only Memory) is a type of non-volatile memory that stores small amounts of data that can be read and written electronically. Factors such as the proliferation of smart devices and IoT (Internet of Things) applications are significantly contributing to this growth.
