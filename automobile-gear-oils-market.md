<p><p>The automobile gear oils market consists of three main types: mineral oil, synthetic oil, and semi-synthetic oil. Synthetic oil, engineered for superior performance, offers better lubrication and temperature resilience, making it ideal for high-performance and modern engines. Semi-synthetic oil blends both types, providing enhanced performance at a lower cost than full synthetics, appealing to consumers seeking a balance between affordability and efficiency.</p></p>
