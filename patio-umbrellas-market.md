<p><p>The patio umbrellas market comprises three main types: wall-mounted umbrellas, center pole umbrellas, and offset pole umbrellas. Wall-mounted umbrellas are fixed to a wall, providing shade without taking up ground space, ideal for smaller areas. Center pole umbrellas feature a central support pole, offering traditional design and stability, suitable for patios and gardens. Offset pole umbrellas have a side-mounted pole, allowing for versatile positioning and shade coverage, making them popular for larger outdoor spaces and dining areas.</p></p>
