<p><strong>The Quartzite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Macrocrystalline Quartzite</li><li>Cryptocrystalline/Microcrystalline Quartzite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quartzite market is primarily segmented into macrocrystalline and cryptocrystalline/microcrystalline types. Macrocrystalline quartzite features larger quartz crystals, providing durability and aesthetic appeal, often used in countertops and flooring. In contrast, cryptocrystalline or microcrystalline quartzite has finer crystal structures, making it ideal for applications requiring a smoother finish, such as decorative stones and tiles. Both types are favored in construction and architecture due to their strength, resistance to weathering, and unique visual characteristics.</p></p>
