<p><p>Construction liability insurance encompasses various market types, including Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance. D&O insurance protects company executives from claims related to their management decisions, safeguarding against legal actions concerning governance and compliance. E&O insurance, on the other hand, covers professionals and firms against claims of negligence or inadequate work, ensuring financial protection against potential lawsuits arising from errors in services provided. Both are essential for risk management in the construction industry.</p></p>
