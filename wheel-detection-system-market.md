In contrast, Double Wheel Sensors monitor two wheels simultaneously, offering enhanced accuracy and robustness in vehicle tracking and monitoring, particularly beneficial in heavy-duty or high-traffic scenarios. Both types serve specific needs in transportation logistics, railway systems, and traffic management.</p></p>
