<p><p>The solid-state sensor market encompasses various types, notably Phased Array and Microelectromechanical Systems (MEMS). Phased Array sensors utilize multiple antennas to detect signals, enhancing imaging and monitoring capabilities, particularly in telecommunications and radar applications. MEMS sensors, on the other hand, integrate mechanical elements with electronic processes on a micrometric scale, enabling precise measurement of physical phenomena like pressure, acceleration, and temperature. Both types are crucial in advancing technology across various sectors, including automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
