<p><strong>The Miniature Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Type</li><li>CCD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature cameras market is primarily categorized into CMOS and CCD types. CMOS cameras utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and compact designs, making them ideal for portable devices. In contrast, CCD cameras, or charge-coupled devices, deliver superior image quality and low-light performance but require more power and are bulkier. Each type serves distinct applications, with CMOS dominating in consumer electronics while CCDs are preferred in professional and scientific imaging scenarios.</p></p>
