<p><p>Smart parking technologies primarily encompass three types of sensors: ultrasonic, radar, and image sensors. Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to detect the presence of vehicles in parking spaces, providing real-time occupancy data. Radar sensors use electromagnetic waves to monitor vehicle movement and space availability, offering high accuracy in various weather conditions. Image sensors, often combined with computer vision, analyze video feeds to identify parked cars and manage space efficiently. Together, these technologies enhance parking management, improve user experience, and optimize urban space utilization.</p></p>
