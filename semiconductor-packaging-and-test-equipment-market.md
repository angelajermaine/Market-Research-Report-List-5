<p><strong>The Semiconductor Packaging and Test Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wafer Probe Station</li><li>Die Bonder</li><li>Dicing Machine</li><li>Test handler</li><li>Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor packaging and test equipment market encompasses various essential types. Wafer probe stations are used for electrically testing semiconductor wafers before packaging. Die bonders attach semiconductor chips to substrates. Dicing machines slice wafers into individual chips or dies. Test handlers automate the process of testing finished devices, improving efficiency. Sorters categorize tested chips based on performance, ensuring quality control. Together, these components play a crucial role in the manufacturing and validation of semiconductor devices, enhancing overall production effectiveness.</p></p>
