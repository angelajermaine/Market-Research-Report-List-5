<p><p>Polyglutamic Acid Urea is a compound derived from the combination of polyglutamic acid (PGA) and urea. PGA is a biodegradable, water-soluble polymer known for its moisturizing and skin-conditioning properties, while urea is a humectant that helps retain moisture and exfoliate dead skin cells. Together, they are widely used in skincare and cosmetic products for their hydrating, anti-aging, and skin-soothing benefits. The compound is also utilized in agricultural applications as a slow-release fertilizer due to its ability to enhance nutrient absorption and improve soil quality.</p><p>The Polyglutamic Acid Urea Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period.
