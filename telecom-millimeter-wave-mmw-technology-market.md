<p><p>Telecom Millimeter Wave (MMW) technology encompasses frequency bands typically above 24 GHz, including V-Band (usually 40-75 GHz) and E-Band (71-86 GHz). V-Band offers high-capacity wireless communication, ideal for short-range applications, while E-Band facilitates ultra-high-speed data transmission over medium distances. Other frequency bands may include W-Band and D-Band, which serve niche markets with specific applications, such as satellite communications and radar systems. The MMW market is driven by increasing demand for high-speed connectivity and data-intensive services.</p></p>
