<p><p>Underwater LED lights are categorized mainly into three types: Thru-Hull Mount, Surface Mount, and Other. Thru-Hull Mount lights are installed through the hull of a boat, providing a seamless look and effective illumination below the waterline. Surface Mount lights attach to the exterior surface, offering easy installation and maintenance. The "Other" category includes specialized or niche products, such as portable or solar-powered lights, catering to diverse applications and user preferences in marine environments.</p></p>
