In contrast, fully automatic grain sorters operate independently, utilizing advanced technologies like sensors and machine learning to sort grains with minimal human involvement. This results in higher throughput, reduced labor costs, and enhanced precision in sorting, catering to diverse agricultural processing needs.</p></p>
