<p><p>The Arc Flash Protection System market consists of two primary types: Arc Flash Detection and Control Systems and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). Arc Flash Detection and Control Systems use sensors and relays to monitor electrical environments, detecting hazardous arc flashes and automatically disconnecting power to mitigate risks. Personal Protective Equipment includes gear such as suits, helmets, gloves, and face shields that protect workers from the thermal and physical hazards associated with an arc flash, enhancing safety during electrical maintenance tasks.</p></p>
