<p><p>The ceramic PTC thermistor market is segmented into two main types: surface mount type and radial leaded type. Surface mount thermistors are designed for compact applications, making them suitable for modern electronic devices where space is a premium. Radial leaded thermistors, on the other hand, feature leads extending from a cylindrical body, allowing for easy integration into larger electronic circuits. Both types offer temperature sensing and overcurrent protection, catering to diverse applications across consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
