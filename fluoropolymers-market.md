<p><p>The fluoropolymers market is segmented into several types, including PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PVDF (Polyvinylidene fluoride), fluoroelastomers, and others. PTFE is renowned for its non-stick properties and high thermal stability, commonly used in cookware and industrial applications. PVDF offers excellent chemical resistance and is often utilized in piping and coatings. Fluoroelastomers provide superior flexibility and resistance to harsh environments, making them ideal for seals and gaskets. "Others" encompass various niche fluoropolymers serving specialized industrial needs.</p></p>
