<p><p>The 4H Silicon Carbide substrates market is segmented primarily into semi-insulating and n-type categories. Semi-insulating substrates are used to minimize parasitic capacitance, enhancing performance in high-frequency and high-voltage applications. Conversely, n-type substrates, characterized by their electron-conducting properties, are crucial for power electronics and high-temperature devices. Each type serves distinct applications in sectors such as automotive, telecommunications, and renewable energy, enabling advancements in efficiency and reliability for next-generation electronic systems and devices.</p></p>
