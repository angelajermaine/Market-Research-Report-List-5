<p><p>The AUV tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires, filled with air, offer flexibility and shock absorption, making them suitable for various terrains. Solid tires, made from durable materials, provide puncture resistance and longevity but are less cushioned. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, offering lightweight, abrasion-resistant options that maintain shape under load. Each type serves specific applications in the automatic underwater vehicles industry, catering to different performance needs and environments.</p></p>
