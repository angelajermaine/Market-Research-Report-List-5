<p><p>The semiconductor CMP materials market is primarily categorized into CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasive, resilient materials used during the chemical-mechanical polishing process to achieve a smooth surface on semiconductor wafers. CMP slurries, on the other hand, are viscous mixtures that contain abrasives and chemicals, facilitating the removal of material from the wafer while ensuring uniformity and precision. Both components play critical roles in semiconductor fabrication, enabling the production of high-performance microelectronics.</p></p>
