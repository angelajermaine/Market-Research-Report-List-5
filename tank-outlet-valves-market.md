<p><p>The Tank Outlet Valves market is primarily categorized into Single Seat and Double Seat valves. Single Seat valves feature a simple design, ideal for applications requiring reliable flow control and minimal leakage. They are typically used in less demanding environments. In contrast, Double Seat valves provide enhanced sealing and are suitable for more critical applications, such as handling aggressive liquids or varying pressure conditions. Their dual seating arrangement allows for better flow regulation and safety, making them more versatile in industrial settings.</p></p>
