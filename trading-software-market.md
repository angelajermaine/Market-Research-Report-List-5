<p><p>Trading software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based trading solutions operate via the internet, allowing users to access them from anywhere, facilitating real-time updates and collaboration. They often offer lower upfront costs and eliminate the need for extensive IT infrastructure. Conversely, on-premises trading software is installed directly on a user's hardware, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
