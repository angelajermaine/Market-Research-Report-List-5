<p><ul><li>Electric retarders</li><li>Hydraulic retarder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric retarders are devices that use an electric motor to generate resistance and slow down a vehicle, typically found in electric or hybrid vehicles. They are known for their efficiency and ability to provide consistent braking power. Hydraulic retarders, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic fluid to create resistance and slow down the vehicle, commonly found in heavy-duty trucks. They are favored for their high braking capacity and ability to handle heavy loads. Both types of retarders are essential components in ensuring safe and reliable braking for vehicles.</p></p>
