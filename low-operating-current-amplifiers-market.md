<p><p>Low operating current amplifiers are categorized into single power supply and dual power supply types based on their power requirements. Single power supply amplifiers use a single voltage source, making them ideal for battery-powered applications due to their efficiency and simplicity. In contrast, dual power supply amplifiers require both positive and negative voltage sources, allowing for greater signal swing and improved performance in more demanding applications. Both types cater to various industries, including consumer electronics and industrial automation.</p></p>
