<p><p>The Metal Cleaning Equipment market is primarily divided into two types: Solvent Metal Cleaning Equipment and Aqueous Metal Cleaning Equipment. Solvent cleaning equipment uses organic solvents to effectively remove oil, grease, and contaminants from metal surfaces, making it suitable for precision parts. In contrast, Aqueous cleaning equipment utilizes water-based solutions, often combined with detergents or surfactants, to clean metals, offering a more environmentally friendly option. Both types serve crucial roles in maintaining metal components for various industrial applications.</p></p>
