<p><p>The Maskless Electron Beam Lithography (EBL) Systems market is characterized by two main types: Gaussian beam EBL systems and shaped beam EBL systems. Gaussian beam EBL systems utilize a focused electron beam with a Gaussian intensity profile, providing high-resolution patterning with straightforward setup. In contrast, shaped beam EBL systems employ an electron beam that is electronically controlled to create complex shapes and patterns more efficiently, enabling faster throughput and greater versatility in nanoscale fabrication applications.</p></p>
