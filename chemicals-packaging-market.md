<p><p>The chemicals packaging market includes various types designed to meet specific needs. Sacks offer lightweight, flexible solutions for powders and granular materials, providing ease of handling and storage. Drums, generally made of metal or plastic, are used for liquids and bulk solids, ensuring sturdiness and protection. FIBCs (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers) are ideal for transporting large quantities of dry bulk chemicals, offering high capacity and durability. Other packaging types may include specialty containers to cater to unique chemical properties or regulatory requirements.</p></p>
