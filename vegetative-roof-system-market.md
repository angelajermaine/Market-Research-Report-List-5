<p><strong>The Vegetative Roof System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extensive Type</li><li>Semi-intensive Type</li><li>Intensive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vegetative roof system market comprises three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive. Extensive roofs feature shallow soil depths and low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants, ideal for lightweight applications. Semi-intensive roofs have deeper substrate layers, supporting a wider variety of plants, requiring moderate maintenance. Intensive roofs offer deeper soil profiles, accommodating diverse landscaping options, including trees and shrubs, but necessitate higher maintenance and structural support. Each type serves different aesthetic and environmental goals, catering to various market needs.</p></p>
