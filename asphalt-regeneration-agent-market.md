<p><strong>The Asphalt Regeneration Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-In-Place Regeneration</li><li>Plant Mix Hot Regeneration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt regeneration agent market encompasses two primary types: Heat-In-Place Regeneration and Plant Mix Hot Regeneration. Heat-In-Place Regeneration involves the application of heat to existing asphalt surfaces, allowing for rejuvenation and restoration on-site, enhancing flexibility and durability. Conversely, Plant Mix Hot Regeneration involves the collection of aged asphalt, which is then processed in a plant with virgin materials and rejuvenators, creating a new mix for road applications. Both methods aim to extend pavement life and improve performance.</p></p>
