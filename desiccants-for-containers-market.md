<p><p>Desiccants for containers are moisture-absorbing agents used to protect products during storage and shipping. Silica gel is the most common type, efficient in absorbing humidity without dissolving, making it ideal for electronics and pharmaceuticals. Calcium chloride offers higher moisture absorption and is often used in bulk packaging or environments with high humidity. Other desiccants may include molecular sieves and activated alumina, each designed for specific applications based on moisture control needs, ensuring product integrity during transit.</p></p>
