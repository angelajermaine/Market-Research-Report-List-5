<p><ul><li>Flat Cam Follower</li><li>Roller Cam Follower</li><li>Needle Cam Follower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cam followers market includes three main types: flat cam followers, roller cam followers, and needle cam followers. Flat cam followers utilize a flat face to engage the cam, offering compact designs and ease of manufacturing. Roller cam followers feature a cylindrical roller that reduces friction and wear, enhancing efficiency and durability. Needle cam followers employ needle bearings for minimal friction and superior load handling capabilities, ideal for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct needs in engine applications, contributing to overall performance and reliability.</p></p>
