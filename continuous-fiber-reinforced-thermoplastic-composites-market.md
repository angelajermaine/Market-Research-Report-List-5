<p><p>Continuous Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Composites (CFRTCs) are advanced materials that incorporate continuous fibers—such as carbon, glass, or aramid—into a thermoplastic matrix. Due to their lightweight nature and recyclability, CFRTCs are gaining traction as sustainable alternatives to traditional materials.</p><p>The Continuous Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Composites Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.10% during the forecast period. Furthermore, rising awareness of environmental sustainability is prompting industries to adopt CFRTCs due to their recyclability and reduced environmental impact. The integration of smart technologies into composite materials, enabling functionalities like self-sensing and damage detection, represents a significant trend.
