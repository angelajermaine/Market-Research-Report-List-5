<p><p>The inulin and fructooligosaccharide market can be categorized into three main types. FOS from sugar is derived through enzymatic processes, offering low-calorie sweetness and digestive benefits. FOS from inulin is produced by partially hydrolyzing inulin, resulting in shorter chains that enhance its sweetness and bioactivity. Each type serves diverse applications in food, beverages, and dietary supplements, catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
