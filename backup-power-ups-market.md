<p><ul><li>DC Power Supply</li><li>AC Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Backup Power UPS market is primarily categorized into two types: DC Power Supply and AC Power Supply. DC Power Supply UPS systems are designed to provide uninterrupted direct current, commonly used in telecommunications and data centers, ensuring critical equipment remains operational during outages. In contrast, AC Power Supply UPS systems convert incoming AC to maintain power for electrical devices, ideal for general applications like computers and industrial equipment. Both types enhance system reliability and protect against data loss during power interruptions.</p></p>
