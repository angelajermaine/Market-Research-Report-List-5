<p><strong>The PVC Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ethylene Process</li><li>Calcium Carbide Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC resins market is primarily categorized into two types based on production processes: the Ethylene Process and the Calcium Carbide Process. The Ethylene Process utilizes ethylene as a raw material, resulting in higher purity and quality, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, the Calcium Carbide Process involves using calcium carbide and acetylene, which are cost-effective and suitable for bulk production, though typically of lower quality. Both processes cater to diverse industries, including construction and automotive.</p></p>
