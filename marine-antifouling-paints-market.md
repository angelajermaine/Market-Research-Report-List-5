<p><strong>The Marine Antifouling Paints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Polishing Copolymer (SPC) Coatings</li><li>Fouling Release Coatings (FRC)</li><li>Ablative Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine antifouling paints are essential for preventing the accumulation of marine organisms on vessels and structures. Self-Polishing Copolymer (SPC) coatings offer controlled release of biocides, maintaining effectiveness over time. Fouling Release Coatings (FRC) feature a smooth surface that makes it difficult for organisms to attach, aiding easy cleaning. Ablative types work by gradually eroding to expose fresh paint, releasing biocides continuously. Other coatings may include non-toxic alternatives or specialty formulations designed for specific environments or applications in maritime settings.</p></p>
