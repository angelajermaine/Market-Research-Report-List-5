<p><ul><li>Sand Form</li><li>Milled Flour Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cristobalite market includes two primary forms: Sand Form and Milled Flour Form. Sand Form consists of coarse, granular particles, commonly used in industrial applications like foundry molds, abrasives, and construction materials due to its durability and heat resistance. Milled Flour Form, a finely ground powder, is utilized in ceramics, paints, and coatings for its smooth texture and enhanced binding properties. Both forms cater to diverse industries, leveraging Cristobalite's unique thermal and chemical stability for specialized applications.</p></p>
