<p><strong>The Automated Aerial Refueling (AAR) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Probe and Drogue Type</li><li>Boom and Receptacle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Aerial Refueling (AAR) systems are categorized primarily into two types: the Probe and Drogue system and the Boom and Receptacle system. The Probe and Drogue type involves a flexible hose and drogue that the receiving aircraft's probe connects to for fuel transfer, commonly used by naval forces. In contrast, the Boom and Receptacle type uses a rigid boom extended from the tanker, inserting into a receptacle on the receiving aircraft, typically favored by air forces for precision and speed.</p></p>
