<p><p>The functionalized polyolefins market comprises various types based on their modification structure. Randomly functionalized polyolefins feature introduced functional groups distributed throughout the polymer chain, enhancing compatibility. End-functionalized polyolefins have specific functional groups at their termini, enabling easier integration with other materials. Block functionalized polyolefins consist of distinct segments with varying functionalities, providing tailored properties. Graft functionalized polyolefins involve side chains attached to the main polymer backbone, enhancing performance and versatility in applications like adhesives, coatings, and packaging materials.</p></p>
