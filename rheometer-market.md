<p><strong>The Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Rheometer</li><li>Capillary Rheometer</li><li>Torque Rheometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rheometer market encompasses various types designed to measure material viscosity and flow characteristics. Dynamic rheometers assess changes in viscosity under oscillatory shear, providing insights into viscoelastic properties. Capillary rheometers measure the flow of materials through narrow passages, suitable for assessing high-viscosity liquids. Torque rheometers evaluate viscosity by measuring the torque required to stir a sample, often used in quality control. Other market types include rotational and stress-controlled rheometers, each serving specific applications in industries like polymers, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
