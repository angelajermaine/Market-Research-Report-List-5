<p><strong>The Retail RFID Security Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HF Tags</li><li>UHF Tags</li><li>LF Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retail RFID security tags market is segmented into three primary types: HF (High Frequency) tags, UHF (Ultra High Frequency) tags, and LF (Low Frequency) tags. HF tags, operating at 13.56 MHz, are commonly used for access control and smart shelving. UHF tags, functioning between 860-960 MHz, offer longer read ranges and are widely employed for inventory tracking and asset management. LF tags, operating at 125-134 kHz, are used in animal tracking and applications requiring close proximity scanning. Each frequency band serves distinct supply chain needs.</p></p>
