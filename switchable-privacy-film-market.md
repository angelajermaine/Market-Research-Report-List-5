<p><p>The switchable privacy film market comprises two main types: non-adhesive film and self-adhesive film. Non-adhesive film is designed to be installed using mechanical means, making it easily removable and ideal for temporary applications. In contrast, self-adhesive film features a sticky backing that allows for straightforward installation on various surfaces, providing permanent or semi-permanent solutions. Both types offer flexibility in privacy management, catering to diverse needs in residential, commercial, and automotive sectors, enhancing comfort and security.</p></p>
