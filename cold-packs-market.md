<p><p>The cold packs market is categorized into two main types: repeatable packs and disposable packs. Repeatable packs are designed for multiple uses, typically featuring gel or ice-reusable materials that can be frozen and applied as needed. In contrast, disposable packs are single-use products, often instant cold packs that activate through a chemical reaction. Both types serve therapeutic purposes, providing pain relief and reducing inflammation, catering to different consumer preferences and applications in medical and recreational settings.</p></p>
