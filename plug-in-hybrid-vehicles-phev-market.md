<p><p>Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles (PHEVs) utilize both a traditional internal combustion engine and an electric motor, allowing flexible driving options. Parallel PHEVs can operate the engine and electric motor simultaneously for enhanced efficiency. Mixed PHEVs offer a blend of both strategies, optimizing performance and electric range. Collectively, these types cater to varying consumer preferences for sustainability and convenience.</p></p>
