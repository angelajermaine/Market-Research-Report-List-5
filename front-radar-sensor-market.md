<p><strong>The Front Radar Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Radar</li><li>Millimeter Wave Radar</li><li>Lidar Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The front radar sensor market encompasses three primary types: Ultrasonic Radar, Millimeter Wave Radar, and Lidar Radar. Ultrasonic Radar uses sound waves within a short range for proximity detection, often in parking assistance systems. Millimeter Wave Radar operates at higher frequencies, providing better accuracy and long-range detection, making it ideal for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Lidar Radar employs laser technology to create precise 3D maps of the environment, enhancing obstacle recognition and vehicle navigation capabilities in autonomous driving applications.</p></p>
