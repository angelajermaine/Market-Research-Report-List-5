<p><ul><li>Powder SOP</li><li>Granular SOP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The potassium sulfate market includes two primary types: powder SOP and granular SOP. Powder SOP is finely milled, offering rapid solubility and quick nutrient release, making it suitable for foliar applications and specialty crops. Conversely, granular SOP features a coarser texture, providing slow-release properties, ideal for bulk application in large agricultural fields. Both forms are valued for their high potassium content, enhancing crop yield, and promoting plant health, catering to diverse agricultural needs and varying application methods.</p></p>
