<p><p>The semiconductor dry etching system market includes three primary types: Chemical Reaction, Physical Removal, and a combination of both. Chemical Reaction etching uses reactive gases to selectively remove material, allowing for precise patterning. Physical Removal relies on physical processes, like ion bombardment, to dislodge material. The Combination approach integrates both methods to optimize etching performance, leveraging the benefits of chemical selectivity and physical efficiency. This diversity enables tailored solutions for various semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
