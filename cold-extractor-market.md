<p><p>Cold extractor market types primarily include physical extraction and chemical extraction. Physical extraction utilizes methods such as cold pressing or solvent-free techniques, preserving the integrity of delicate compounds while extracting oils and essences from natural sources. In contrast, chemical extraction employs solvents and reagents to isolate specific components, often resulting in higher yields but potential alteration of the product's natural properties. Both methods are essential in various industries, including food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, to obtain high-quality extracts.</p></p>
