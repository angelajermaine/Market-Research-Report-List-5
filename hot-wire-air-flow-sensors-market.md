<p><p>Hot Wire Air Flow Sensors are precision instruments used to measure the flow rate of air or gas. They operate based on the principle of heat transfer, where a thin wire heated to a specific temperature cools down as air flows over it. The change in temperature correlates directly with the flow speed, enabling accurate flow measurement. These sensors are widely used in automotive, HVAC, and industrial applications due to their high sensitivity and fast response times.</p><p>The Hot Wire Air Flow Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.90% during the forecast period. Emerging trends include the development of miniaturized sensors for compact applications and the incorporation of digital outputs for enhanced data analytics.
