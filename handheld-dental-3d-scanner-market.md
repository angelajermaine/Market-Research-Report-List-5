<p><p>The handheld dental 3D scanner market can be categorized based on the light source technology used: laser and LED light sources. Laser-based scanners utilize focused beams of light to capture precise details of dental structures, offering high accuracy and depth of field. In contrast, LED light sources provide rapid scanning capabilities, using structured light to create detailed 3D models. Both technologies enhance dental diagnostics and treatment planning, with variations in speed, precision, and cost, catering to different clinical needs.</p></p>
