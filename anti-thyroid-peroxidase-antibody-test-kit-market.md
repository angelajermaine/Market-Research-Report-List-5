<p><p>The Anti-thyroid Peroxidase Antibody Test Kit market consists of two main types: Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) and Time-resolved Immunoassay (TRIA). CLIA employs light-emitting reactions to detect antibodies, offering high sensitivity and rapid results. TRIA utilizes time-resolved fluorescence, allowing for precise quantification of antibodies through controlled measurements. Both methods play a crucial role in diagnosing thyroid disorders, such as Hashimoto's thyroiditis, by assessing autoimmune responses, enabling effective management and treatment of thyroid-related conditions.</p></p>
