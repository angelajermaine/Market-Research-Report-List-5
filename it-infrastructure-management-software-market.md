<p><p>IT Infrastructure Management Software can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed directly on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant maintenance and upfront costs. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted remotely and accessed via the internet, providing scalability, flexibility, and lower initial expenditures. Organizations may choose between these types based on their specific operational needs, budget, and desire for control over their IT environment.</p></p>
