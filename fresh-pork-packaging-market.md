<p><p>The Fresh Pork Packaging market encompasses various types, including Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP), which enhances shelf life by altering the gas composition inside the package. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly conforms to the product, providing excellent protection and a longer shelf life. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) involves shaping material around the product and sealing it under vacuum, ensuring freshness. Other methods may include traditional packaging techniques, shrink wrapping, and modified polymer bags, catering to diverse consumer and retailer needs.</p></p>
