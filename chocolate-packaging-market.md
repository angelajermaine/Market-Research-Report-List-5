<p><p>The chocolate packaging market can be categorized into rigid and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging includes materials like plastic, glass, and metal, providing durability and protection, ideal for premium chocolates and gifting. It often features unique shapes and designs to enhance shelf appeal. In contrast, flexible packaging utilizes materials such as films and pouches, offering lighter weight and convenience. This type is commonly used for standard chocolate bars and snacks, allowing for easy storage and transportation while maintaining product freshness.</p></p>
