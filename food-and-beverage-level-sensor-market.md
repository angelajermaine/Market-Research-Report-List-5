<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contact-Less</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food and Beverage Level Sensor market is categorized into two main types: contact and contactless sensors. Contact sensors directly measure the level of liquids by making physical contact with the substance, offering accurate readings but may require regular maintenance. In contrast, contactless sensors utilize technologies such as ultrasonic, infrared, or radar to determine liquid levels without direct contact, providing a hygienic solution ideal for food and beverage applications. Each type serves specific needs depending on operational requirements and hygiene standards.</p></p>
