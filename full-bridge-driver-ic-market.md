<p><p>The Full Bridge Driver IC market consists of Dual and Quad Full-Bridge Driver IC types, each offering distinct functionalities for driving motors and actuators. Dual Full-Bridge Driver ICs can control two motors simultaneously, making them ideal for applications requiring compact design and efficiency, such as robotics. Conversely, Quad Full-Bridge Driver ICs can manage four motors, suitable for more complex systems like multi-axis robots or advanced automation setups. Both types enhance performance and reliability while optimizing space in electronic designs.</p></p>
