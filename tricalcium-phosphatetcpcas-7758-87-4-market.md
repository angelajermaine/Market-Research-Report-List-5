<p><p>Tricalcium Phosphate (TCP), identified by CAS 7758-87-4, is a calcium phosphate compound utilized in various applications, particularly in the biomedical and food industries. The market can be categorized into two types: Low Temperature β-TCP and High Temperature α-TCP. Low Temperature β-TCP exhibits better bioactivity and is primarily used in bone grafts and dental applications due to its ability to promote cell attachment and growth. Conversely, High Temperature α-TCP has enhanced stability and is often used in ceramics and cement applications.</p></p>
