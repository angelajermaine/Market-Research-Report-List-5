<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chucks for Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic chucks are devices used to hold wafers in place during semiconductor manufacturing processes. The Coulomb type uses static electricity generated by high-voltage electrodes to create a strong adhesive force, ensuring secure wafer placement. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type operates on a different principle, utilizing a combination of electrostatic and mechanical forces to achieve wafer clamping. Both types are essential in enhancing precision and efficiency in wafer handling, impacting the overall performance of semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
