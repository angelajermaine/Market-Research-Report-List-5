<p><p>Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) sensors are critical for monitoring and regulating engine performance and emissions. The market is primarily divided into two types: PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) sensors. PTC sensors exhibit an increase in resistance with rising temperature, offering high stability, while NTC sensors demonstrate a decrease in resistance as temperature rises, providing rapid response times. Each type serves distinct applications in automotive and industrial sectors, impacting efficiency and safety in engine operations.</p></p>
