<p><p>The Steel Fiber Reinforced Concrete market comprises several types based on fiber production methods. Cut Wire (Cold Drawn) Steel Fibers are made by cutting wire rods, providing high tensile strength. Slit Sheet Steel Fibers are derived from slitting steel sheets, offering uniform dispersion in concrete. Melt Extract Steel Fibers are produced by melting and extracting steel, ensuring consistency. Mill Cut Steel Fibers are created by cutting steel from rolls or sheets, facilitating easier incorporation into concrete mixes for improved performance and durability.</p></p>
