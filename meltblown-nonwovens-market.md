<p><p>Meltblown nonwovens are classified into two primary market types: fine fiber meltblown nonwovens and dual texture meltblown nonwovens. Fine fiber meltblown nonwovens feature ultra-thin fibers, enhancing filtration and barrier properties, making them ideal for applications like medical masks and filters. In contrast, dual texture meltblown nonwovens combine different fiber diameters or textures, allowing for versatile use in products requiring both softness and strength, such as hygiene products and industrial wipes. Both types play crucial roles in various industries.</p></p>
