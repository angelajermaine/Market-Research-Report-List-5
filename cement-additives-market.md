<p><p>The cement additives market comprises three main types: chemical, mineral, and fiber additives. Chemical additives enhance properties such as workability, setting time, and strength of cement. Mineral additives, like fly ash and slag, improve durability and reduce permeability while also being environmentally friendly. Fiber additives, made from materials like steel or polypropylene, reinforce cement, increasing its tensile strength and resistance to cracking. Together, these additives optimize cement performance for various construction applications, promoting efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
