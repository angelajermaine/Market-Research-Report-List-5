<p><p>The supermarket anti-theft device market includes three main types: Radio Frequency (RF), Acousto-Magnetic (AM), and Electromagnetic Wave (EM). RF systems utilize tags that emit signals, triggering alarms at exit points when deactivated tags are not detected. AM devices operate with magnetic fields and are resistant to certain interference, enhancing security. EM systems work on electromagnetic principles, often using simpler technology but with wider detection ranges. Each type offers varying levels of security, installation complexity, and cost, catering to different retail needs.</p></p>
