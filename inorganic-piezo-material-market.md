<p><p>The inorganic piezo material market primarily includes two types: piezoelectric crystals and piezoelectric ceramics. Piezoelectric crystals, such as quartz, exhibit direct piezoelectric effects and are known for their high sensitivity and durability, making them suitable for precision applications. In contrast, piezoelectric ceramics, like lead zirconate titanate (PZT), are widely used due to their high piezoelectric coefficients and versatility in various applications, including sensors, actuators, and transducers. Both types play essential roles in advancing technology in various industries.</p></p>
