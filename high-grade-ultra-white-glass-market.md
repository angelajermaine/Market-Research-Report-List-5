<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High-grade Ultra White Glass market predominantly comprises two types: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in a smooth surface and uniform thickness, ideal for architectural applications. In contrast, Float Glass is manufactured by floating molten glass on molten tin, creating a flat and distortion-free surface. Both types are valued for their high purity and clarity, making them suitable for premium applications in construction, automotive, and optical industries.</p></p>
