<p><p>The Color Crystal Panel market comprises three primary types: Glass, Acrylic, and PC (Polycarbonate) panels. Glass panels offer excellent optical clarity and durability but are heavier and more fragile. Acrylic panels are lightweight and impact-resistant, making them suitable for various applications, though they may be less scratch-resistant. PC panels provide high impact resistance and UV protection, ideal for outdoor use. Each type caters to different aesthetic and functional needs, distinguishing itself in design flexibility, strength, and application suitability.</p></p>
