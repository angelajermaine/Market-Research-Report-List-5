<p><ul><li>Hot-Melt Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction tape adhesive market consists of three primary types: hot-melt based, solvent-based, and others. Hot-melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that bond upon cooling, offering quick adhesion and versatility for varying surfaces. Solvent-based adhesives use volatile solvents to create strong bonds and are ideal for heavy-duty applications due to their durability. The "others" category encompasses a range of specialized adhesives, including water-based and reactive formulations, catering to niche needs in construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
