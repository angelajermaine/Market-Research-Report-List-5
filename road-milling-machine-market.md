<p><p>Road milling machines are categorized into two main types based on their mobility: wheeled and tracked. Wheeled road milling machines are characterized by their mobility and are ideal for urban settings, offering flexibility and ease of transport over short distances. In contrast, tracked road milling machines provide superior stability and traction on uneven surfaces, making them suitable for larger-scale projects and rough terrains. Both types are essential for efficient asphalt and concrete removal in road maintenance and construction.</p></p>
