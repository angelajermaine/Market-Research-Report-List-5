<p><strong>The LDPE Resins for Extrusion Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular Process</li><li>Autoclave Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LDPE resins for extrusion coating are categorized into two main types based on processing methods: the tubular process and the autoclave process. The tubular process involves melting resin pellets and extruding them through a die to form a continuous tube, which is then flattened for coating applications. In contrast, the autoclave process utilizes a pressurized environment for polymerization, resulting in high-density films with enhanced properties. Both methods cater to different product requirements, impacting applications in packaging and other industries.</p></p>
