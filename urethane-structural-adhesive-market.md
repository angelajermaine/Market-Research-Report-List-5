<p><p>The urethane structural adhesive market is divided into two main types: single-component and two-component adhesives. Single-component adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that cure when exposed to moisture or air, making them convenient and easy to use, ideal for applications requiring efficiency. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed before application, providing superior strength and durability for demanding environments. Both types offer unique benefits, catering to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
