<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Dispersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminium oxide nanomaterial market is primarily segmented into two types: solid and dispersion. Solid aluminium oxide nanomaterials are typically in bulk form and are used in applications such as abrasives, catalysts, and ceramic products due to their high strength and thermal stability. In contrast, dispersion aluminium oxide nanomaterials are formulated as stable suspensions in liquids, enabling their use in coatings, composites, and cosmetic products. Each type offers unique properties tailored for specific industrial applications, enhancing performance and functionality.</p></p>
