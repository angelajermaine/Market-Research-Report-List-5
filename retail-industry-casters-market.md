<p><p>The retail industry caster market comprises two primary types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters allow 360-degree movement, providing enhanced maneuverability and flexibility, making them ideal for shopping carts and display units. Rigid casters, on the other hand, move in a straight line, offering stability and support for heavier loads, often used in shelving and storage solutions. Together, these caster types facilitate efficient transportation and mobility of goods within retail environments, ensuring smooth operations and customer satisfaction.</p></p>
