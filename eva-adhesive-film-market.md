<p><p>The EVA adhesive film market is primarily categorized into two types: transparent EVA film and white EVA film. Transparent EVA film is utilized in applications requiring optical clarity, such as in packaging and laminating processes, offering excellent adhesion and flexibility. In contrast, white EVA film is often used for applications that prioritize opacity, providing a barrier against UV light and enhancing aesthetic appeal. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction, contributing to their growing demand.</p></p>
