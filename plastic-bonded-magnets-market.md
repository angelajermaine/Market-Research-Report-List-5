<p><p>Plastic bonded magnets are composite materials made by mixing magnetic powders with a polymer binder, offering flexibility in design and manufacturing. **Plastic Injected Magnets** are produced by injecting a magnetic compound into molds, enabling complex shapes and high precision, ideal for small, intricate components. **Plastic Pressed Magnets** are formed by compressing magnetic powder and polymer mixtures under high pressure, resulting in stronger magnetic properties and suitability for larger, robust applications. Both types are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective for diverse industries.</p></p>
