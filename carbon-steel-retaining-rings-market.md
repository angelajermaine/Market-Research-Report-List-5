<p><ul><li>External Retaining Rings</li><li>Internal Retaining Rings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon steel retaining rings market comprises two main types: external retaining rings and internal retaining rings. External retaining rings fit over a shaft or component, securing parts in place by providing a shoulder against which they can rest. Internal retaining rings, on the other hand, are designed to fit inside a bore or housing to secure components from falling out. Both types are essential in various applications, including automotive, machinery, and industrial equipment, ensuring reliable assembly and operation.</p></p>
