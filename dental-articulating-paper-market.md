<p><p>Dental articulating paper is used in dentistry to assess occlusion and analyze bite force during patient examinations. The market includes two primary types: horseshoe and straight strip. Horseshoe articulating paper features a curved design, allowing for positioning in various areas of the mouth, facilitating easier access for bite checks. In contrast, straight strip articulating paper offers a linear shape, ideal for uniform applications and specific areas. Both types are essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning in dental practices.</p></p>
