<p><ul><li>Low Leak Ear Simulator</li><li>High Leak Ear Simulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-acoustic ear simulator market is categorized into two main types: low leak and high leak ear simulators. Low leak ear simulators are designed to closely mimic the natural ear's acoustic properties, minimizing sound leakage and providing accurate measurements for hearing aid testing and development. In contrast, high leak ear simulators allow for more sound escape, simulating specific real-world scenarios where acoustic isolation is less critical. Both types serve essential roles in assessing audio performance and ensuring optimal sound quality in hearing devices.</p></p>
