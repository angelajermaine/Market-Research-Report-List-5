GIS (Geographic Information Systems) integrates spatial data for analysis and visualization. LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) provides precise elevation data through laser scanning. Digital orthophotography combines aerial imagery with corrected geographic references, while aerial photography captures images from aircraft for mapping purposes.
