<p><p>Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC) are a type of fuel cell that utilizes a solid polymer membrane as an electrolyte. This technology converts chemical energy from hydrogen and oxygen into electrical energy, with water and heat as the only byproducts.
