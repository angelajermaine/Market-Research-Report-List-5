<p><p>The single-to-double stroller market comprises three main types: side-by-sides, in-lines, and convertibles. Side-by-side strollers feature two seats positioned next to each other, offering equal space for both children and easy interaction. In-line strollers, or tandem models, have one seat in front of the other, allowing a more compact design for navigating tight spaces. Convertibles can transition from a single to a double stroller configuration, making them versatile for growing families and accommodating varying needs over time.</p></p>
