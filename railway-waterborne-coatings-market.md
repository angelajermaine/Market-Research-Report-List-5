<p><p>Railway Waterborne Coatings are specialized paint products designed for use in the railway industry, primarily for the protection and aesthetic enhancement of trains and rail infrastructure. These coatings are formulated to provide excellent adhesion, durability, and resistance to harsh environmental conditions, including UV exposure and corrosion. Additionally, the expansion of rail networks globally and the rise in freight and passenger transport are boosting demand for high-quality coatings.
