<p><p>The explosives detection scanner market features several key technologies. Gamma-ray radiography utilizes gamma rays to penetrate materials and identify explosives based on their atomic signatures. X-ray radiography employs X-rays to create images of objects, allowing for the detection of concealed explosives by revealing density differences. Muon tomography leverages cosmic muons to gather information about the internal structure of objects, detecting anomalies associated with explosives. Each technology offers unique advantages for security applications in various environments, enhancing safety and threat assessment.</p></p>
