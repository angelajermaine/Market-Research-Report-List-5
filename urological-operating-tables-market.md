<p><p>The urological operating tables market comprises various types categorized by their mechanism of operation. Electric tables utilize motors for height and positioning adjustments, providing precise control and ease of use. Hydraulic tables rely on fluid systems for adjustments, offering stability and strength but often requiring manual intervention. Electro-hydraulic tables combine both electric and hydraulic elements for enhanced functionality and versatility. Manual tables depend on human force for adjustments, offering simplicity but less convenience than powered options.</p></p>
