<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) wheel speed sensor market primarily comprises two types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed readings essential for advanced driver assistance systems and stability control. Meanwhile, magnetic electric sensors generate voltage in response to changes in magnetic fields as wheels turn, offering reliable performance in various conditions. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety, efficiency, and overall driving experience in NEVs.</p></p>
