<p><p>The waterproof abrasive paper market primarily consists of two types: waterproof latex paper and waterproof kraft paper. Waterproof kraft paper, on the other hand, is typically made from treated kraft fibers, providing excellent tear resistance and water resistance, suitable for heavy-duty sanding tasks. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing surface preparation and finishing processes.</p></p>
