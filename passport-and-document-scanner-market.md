<p><p>The passport and document scanner market encompasses various technologies, including RFID, barcode, and OCR. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) provides automated scanning and quick access to data from electronic passports. Barcodes enable fast identification and tracking of documents through printed codes. OCR (Optical Character Recognition) converts scanned images of text into machine-readable data, enhancing data extraction and processing. Together, these technologies improve efficiency and accuracy in managing passport and document verification processes across various sectors, including travel and security.</p></p>
