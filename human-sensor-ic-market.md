<p><p>The human sensor IC market includes various types, primarily focusing on Pyroelectric Infrared (PIR) sensors and Thermopile sensors. PIR sensors detect motion by sensing temperature changes from moving bodies, making them ideal for security and automation applications. In contrast, Thermopile sensors measure absolute temperature variations, enabling contactless temperature monitoring and more extensive thermal analysis. Both sensor types play crucial roles in smart home technologies, HVAC systems, and industrial automation, enhancing energy efficiency and user comfort through accurate human presence detection.</p></p>
