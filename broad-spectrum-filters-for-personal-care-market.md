<p><p>Broad spectrum filters in the personal care market are categorized into water phase and oil phase products. Water phase filters are designed to be soluble in aqueous formulations, providing effective UV protection in products like lotions and gels. In contrast, oil phase filters are formulated for emollient-based products, where they integrate into creams and oils, ensuring stable UV absorption in formulations. Both types work synergistically to offer comprehensive sun protection, catering to diverse consumer preferences and skin types.</p></p>
