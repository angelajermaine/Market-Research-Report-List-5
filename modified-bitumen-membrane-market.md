<p><p>The Modified Bitumen Membrane market primarily consists of two types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS membranes offer flexibility and elasticity, making them suitable for environments with significant temperature fluctuations. In contrast, APP membranes are known for their durability and UV resistance, making them ideal for flat roofs and areas exposed to sunlight. Both types provide effective waterproofing solutions, but their distinct properties cater to different application needs in construction and roofing industries.</p></p>
