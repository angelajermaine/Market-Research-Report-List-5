Complete plant proteins contain all nine essential amino acids needed for optimal body function, typically sourced from quinoa, soy, and chia seeds. In contrast, incomplete plant proteins lack one or more essential amino acids and are often derived from legumes, grains, and vegetables. Consumers seek these products for health benefits, dietary preferences, and sustainable protein options, influencing the growth and diversity of the market.</p></p>
