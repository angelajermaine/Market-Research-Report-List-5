<p><p>The gasoline detergent market is primarily categorized into PIBA-based, PEA-based, and other formulations. PIBA-based detergents, derived from polyisobutylene amine, are known for their superior cleaning properties and ability to prevent deposits in fuel injectors. PEA-based detergents, or polyether amines, offer excellent thermal stability and oxidation resistance, enhancing engine performance. The "Others" category includes alternative formulations that may focus on specific properties or regional preferences. Together, these variants enhance fuel efficiency, reduce emissions, and improve overall engine health.</p></p>
