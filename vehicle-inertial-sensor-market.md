<p><p>Vehicle Inertial Sensors are advanced devices that measure the motion and orientation of a vehicle using inertial measurement units (IMUs). They play a vital role in various applications, including advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS), autonomous driving, and electronic stability control systems.</p><p>The Vehicle Inertial Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.70% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demands for safety features and advancements in automotive technologies. Key trends include the rising popularity of automated driving solutions and the demand for enhanced automotive safety standards, pushing manufacturers to innovate and improve sensor capabilities. IMUs integrate both acceleration and angular velocity data, enabling comprehensive motion tracking and improved inertial navigation. Together, these sensors enhance vehicle safety, performance, and autonomy, making them essential for advanced driver-assistance systems and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
