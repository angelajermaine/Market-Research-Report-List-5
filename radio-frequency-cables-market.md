<p><p>The radio frequency cables market is divided primarily into two key types based on impedance: 50Ω and 75Ω. The 50Ω cables are commonly used in applications requiring efficient power delivery, such as in telecommunications and broadcasting. In contrast, 75Ω cables are typically favored for transmission of video signals, including in cable television and satellite systems, due to their superior performance in minimizing signal loss. Both types serve distinct markets, influenced by their specific technical requirements and applications.</p></p>
